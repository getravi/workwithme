---
name: debug-error
description: Systematically diagnose and fix errors or unexpected behavior. Use when the user reports a bug, error message, or unexpected output.
---

When debugging, follow this process:
1. Reproduce the error — identify the exact inputs and conditions
2. Identify the root cause — trace the error to its source, not just the symptom
3. Propose a fix — explain why it solves the root cause
4. Verify — describe how to confirm the fix works
